Enviroment & Sanitation.

The overall purpose of this programme is to protect human health through environmental conservation initiatives i.e through planting of trees, distribution of masks to help fight the Covid-19 pandemic.

Sports, Art & Culture

The programme provides us with a shared set of attitudes, values, goals and practices that define us as a group of people within our various societies.

About

The Elimu HUB Kisumu project in Migosi Estate is aimed at supporting voluntary and community based initiatives in Kisumu and the region at large, in the sectors of Technology,Enviroment,Sports,Arts, Culture and Education. Elimu hub works to create a sustainable enviroment whereby its individual members and community groups can benefit from the use ICT, Enviroment and Socio-economic activities.
